Common Questions

STR Investment FAQ

Can I legally Airbnb my condo in Old Town Scottsdale?

Yes, but it depends on the specific property and HOA. Scottsdale allows short-term rentals in most residential zones, but individual HOAs may have restrictions. We can help you identify STR-eligible properties before you buy.

What's the average ROI on an Old Town STR?

Top-performing Old Town STRs typically see 6-10% cap rates, with annual gross revenues ranging from $50K-$100K+ depending on the property type, location, and management approach.

Do I need a permit for a short-term rental in Scottsdale?

Yes. Arizona requires STR operators to register with the Arizona Department of Revenue and obtain a Transaction Privilege Tax license. Scottsdale also has local registration requirements.

Which buildings are best for STR investment?

This depends on your budget and goals. We maintain a quarterly ranking of top-performing STR buildings based on revenue, occupancy, and guest satisfaction. Contact us for the latest list.
