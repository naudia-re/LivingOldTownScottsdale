The Approach

It's not a transaction. It's a strategy.

Buying in Old Town isn't just searching the MLS. It's knowing which buildings allow STRs, which streets flood, which builders age well, and which sellers are quietly open to off-market offers. We work the week so you don't have to.

The Search

How searching with us actually works

Searching the public MLS will only get you so far. Here's what changes when we run the search alongside you.

01 · Saved Search

Built Around Your Criteria

We build a custom MLS search around your priorities — neighborhoods, beds/baths, lot size, year built, STR-eligibility — and you get pinged the moment something matches.

02 · Off-Market Access

Inventory You Won't See Online

Compass Coming Soons, pocket listings, and the Selling Sonoran network of agents and homeowners. Most buyers never see this layer.

03 · Curated Showings

Private Tours, On Your Schedule

We pre-walk homes when it makes sense so you only spend Saturdays on the ones worth seeing. Group runs and one-off private tours both work.

04 · Out-of-State

Live FaceTime & Video Tours

Relocators, snowbirds, and STR investors get full FaceTime walkthroughs, drone footage when it matters, and detailed condition videos before you fly in.

05 · Weekly Check-In

Recalibrate as the Market Moves

A recurring 15-minute call to refine criteria, review new listings, and adjust the plan as inventory shifts. Markets move — the search should too.

06 · Vendor Network

Lenders, Inspectors, Designers

Vetted relationships across lending, inspection, contracting, and design so you never start from scratch. Every recommendation is one we'd use ourselves.
