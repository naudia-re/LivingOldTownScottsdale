About Living Old Town Scottsdale

A resource built on local knowledge, honest perspective, and a genuine love for Old Town Scottsdale.

The Brand

More Than Real Estate

Living Old Town Scottsdale was created to tell the story of one of Arizona's most dynamic neighborhoods — from the streets locals walk every day to the developments shaping its future.

We go beyond the glossy highlights to share what it's really like to live, work, invest, and build a life in Old Town. Through hyper-local storytelling, neighborhood guides, development updates, and lifestyle features, LOTS connects residents, buyers, business owners, and visitors to the heart of the community.

Our content is grounded, informative, and human — designed to educate, inspire, and help people make confident decisions about where they live and how they experience Scottsdale.

Living Old Town Scottsdale is not a sales platform — it's a resource. A place for honest insight, local perspective, and meaningful storytelling.

Founder & Lead Agent

Naudia Andrescavage

Naudia is a Scottsdale-based real estate professional and the founder of both Living Old Town Scottsdale and the Selling Sonoran Group at Compass. With deep roots in Old Town and years of experience in real estate, marketing, and community engagement, she created LOTS to be the resource she wished existed.

Her approach is simple: lead with value, build genuine relationships, and always put the client's interests first. Whether you're buying your first condo, investing in a short-term rental, or selling a family home, Naudia brings the same level of care, market knowledge, and strategic thinking to every transaction.
