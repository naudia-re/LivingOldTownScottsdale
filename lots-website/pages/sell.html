Step One

What's your home actually worth?

Most online estimates miss by 6–10% in Old Town because they don't account for floor plan, lot, view, or recent upgrades. We use a Lofty-powered automated valuation as a starting point, then layer in a human Comparative Market Analysis based on the actual comps and the current market.

The automated valuation runs in under a minute. Enter your address on our Selling Sonoran home-value tool and you'll get an instant estimate based on recent sales, current inventory, and the algorithm's read on your specific block.

The human valuation is where Old Town pricing actually lives. We walk the home, factor in the upgrades the algorithm can't see, compare against the most relevant active listings, and price for the buyer most likely to write the strongest offer.

The Marketing

How we actually market your home

No template flyer, no MLS-and-pray. Every Old Town listing gets a custom marketing plan that starts the day we sign the listing agreement and runs through close.

Editorial Photography

Magazine-grade interior and exterior photography. Twilight shots on the homes that warrant it. Every Old Town listing deserves the same attention a Compass luxury listing gets in Manhattan.

Cinematic Walkthrough Video

A polished 60–90 second tour film that lives on the property website and powers social-media ads. Buyers who watch the video are 3–4× more likely to request a private showing.

Drone & Aerial

Drone footage of the lot, neighborhood context, and proximity to Old Town landmarks. Especially valuable for single-family homes with pools, larger lots, or premium locations.

Coming Soon Strategy

Compass Private Exclusives and Coming Soon listings build buyer demand before the home hits the MLS. By the time it's live, qualified buyers are already booked for first-day showings.

Custom Property Website

Every listing gets a dedicated single-property website with photos, video, floor plans, neighborhood guide, and a direct contact form. Used in every social and email touchpoint.

Targeted Social Ads

Geo-targeted Instagram, Facebook, and TikTok ad campaigns. Audiences modeled around relocators, second-home buyers, STR investors, and luxury-segment buyers active in Old Town.

Compass Agent Email Blast

Your listing is distributed inside the Compass agent network at Coming Soon and on go-live — surfacing to the agents working with relocators moving into Scottsdale right now.

Neighborhood Mailers

A printed listing flyer mailed to the surrounding blocks. Neighbors often know the next buyer — an out-of-state family member, a friend who's been watching the neighborhood, or a renter ready to buy.

Open Houses & Brokers' Tour

Strategic public open houses for the right listings, plus a private brokers' open inside the first week so Scottsdale's top buyer agents see the home before their clients do.

FAQ

Selling in Old Town, answered

The questions we get from every seller before they sign the listing agreement. Honest answers, not the brochure version.

How is my home priced?
Pricing is set with a custom Comparative Market Analysis using the most recent comparable sales, the active listings your home will be competing against, your home's specific upgrades, and the seasonal pacing of the Old Town market. We pair the human CMA with a Lofty-powered automated valuation so you have both data points to work from — and we'll explain exactly how we got to the recommended list price.
What is Compass Concierge and how does it work?
Compass Concierge fronts the cost of pre-listing improvements — staging, painting, light renovation, deep cleaning, landscaping — with zero interest and no upfront cost. We assess what's worth doing during the consultation, manage the vendors, and you pay it back at closing out of the proceeds. We typically lift sale price by 4–12% on the homes that need it.
How long does it take to sell in Old Town Scottsdale?
It varies with price band, building, and season — but well-priced and well-marketed Old Town homes are typically under contract in 14–45 days. Many of our condo listings sell before they hit the MLS through Compass Private Exclusives, which finds the buyer during the Coming Soon phase.
What does your marketing actually include?
Editorial photography, cinematic walkthrough video, drone aerials, a custom single-property website, printed listing flyer mailed to the surrounding blocks, Compass Private Exclusives and Coming Soon network exposure, targeted Instagram/Facebook/TikTok ads, an email blast to ~30,000 Compass agents nationally, a private brokers' open, and strategic public open houses. All custom-built per listing — no template plans.
Do you do open houses?
Yes — when they make sense. We always host a private brokers' open inside the first week so Scottsdale's top buyer agents see the home first. Public open houses get scheduled strategically based on price band, building type, and how the listing is pacing — not just because it's Saturday.
What's the commission structure?
Commission is negotiated at the listing consultation and depends on price band, complexity, and the level of pre-listing work. We're transparent about every dollar — you'll see the full breakdown before you sign, plus a side-by-side of what Compass Concierge improvements would cost vs. the projected price lift.
What if my home doesn't sell?
We reset together. Review showing feedback and online behavior, refresh photography or staging if anything has slipped, recalibrate price against the live market, and re-launch with a refreshed strategy. Most homes that initially miss simply need a recalibration, not a different agent — and we stay accountable through that work.
Can you help me find my next home, too?
Absolutely — most of our sellers also use us as their buyer's agent for the next move. Compass Bridge Loans help bridge the timing gap if you need to buy your next home before this one closes, and we coordinate both transactions to land on the dates that actually work for you.
