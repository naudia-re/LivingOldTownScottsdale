Scottsdale Estates
Mid-Century • No HOA • Walkable to Old Town

The most sought-after single-family neighborhood near Old Town. Multiple subdivisions (Estates 1-11) of mid-century ranch homes built in the 1950s-60s, many extensively remodeled with modern updates. No HOA means freedom to renovate. A short walk or bike ride to El Dorado Park, the Greenbelt, and Old Town's core.

Papago Parkway
Close to Papago Park • No HOA • Scottsdale + Tempe Access

Southwest of Old Town near the Papago Park boundary, offering mid-century ranch homes with generous lots and no HOA. Many homes have been fully remodeled. Unique positioning gives easy access to both Old Town Scottsdale and Tempe, with Papago Golf Course and the park's hiking trails minutes away.

Vista Del Camino
Family-Friendly • Park Access • Value Buy

A peaceful, family-oriented neighborhood centered around Vista del Camino Park with its fishing lake, disc golf, and playgrounds. Adjacent to the Indian Bend Wash Greenbelt, these mid-century block homes from the 1960s-70s offer strong value for the Scottsdale market and a quieter pace of life.

Park Scottsdale
Starter Homes • Townhomes • Most Affordable

One of the most budget-friendly entry points into Scottsdale. A mix of single-family homes and townhomes from the 1970s-80s between Thomas and McDowell Roads. Popular with first-time buyers and investors looking for a Scottsdale address at a more accessible price point.

South Scottsdale & Hy-View
Young Professionals • Mountain Views • Strong Reno Market

South of Old Town toward the Tempe border, this area attracts young professionals and families with a vibrant renovation scene. The Hy-View pocket is known for larger lots and scenic mountain views. About a mile from Old Town's core with access to the 11-mile Indian Bend Wash Greenbelt.

Indian Bend
Diverse Mix • Greenbelt Access • Central Location

A diverse blend of residential subdivisions between Camelback and Indian Bend Roads. Ranges from modest townhomes to luxury estates, with multiple sub-communities and direct access to the Indian Bend Wash Greenbelt. Close to Continental Golf Course and centrally located for all of Scottsdale.

McCormick Ranch
Master-Planned • Lakes • Golf • Bike Paths

One of Scottsdale's most established master-planned communities with over 67 subdivisions. Known for 10 lakes, 25+ miles of bike paths, two golf courses, and mature landscaping. The southern edge is about 3-4 miles north of Old Town. Includes waterfront homes, golf course properties, and patio homes.

Villa Monterey
55+ Active Adult • Walkable to Fashion Square • Low Maintenance

An 800-home active adult (55+) community along the Chaparral Road corridor. Built 1961-1969 with attached townhomes and condos. Walking distance to Fashion Square Mall. Community pool, spa, clubhouse, tennis courts, and walking trails. Resale-only market with affordable entry points for Scottsdale.

Continental Golf Course Area
Golf Course Living • Mix of Styles • Central

Surrounding the Continental Golf Club (a par-60 executive course open to the public), this area offers a mix of single-family homes, patio homes, and resort-style condos along the fairways. Central location with good walkability and access to multiple commercial areas.
